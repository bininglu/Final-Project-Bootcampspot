New York City has one of the most widely-used bike sharing programs in the world. There are around 6,000 bike stations in NYC alone. While the city already has an extensive public transit system throughout the five boroughs, there is a dedicated ridership in some parts of New York City where bikes prove to be the better mode of transportation. With a population of roughly 8.5 million people, it is worth having alternative ways to get around.
