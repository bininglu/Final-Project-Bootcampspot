Chicago

Like NYC, Chicago has a reliable transit system that most residents use regularly. The program launched in 2013 with 75 stations across the city, and the number of stations grew as bikesharing in Chicago became popular. There was data that was scraped from both Yelp and TripAdvisor which allowed for a comprehensive analysis of the Divvy ridership. With roughly 2.7 million residents, Chicago has seen a surge and loyalty in its bikeshare program.
